Companion Name: Rafayel: Fresh Paint
Tags: Heal | Single Target
How To Obtain: ???
Basic Protocore Build Notes: 
- Attack-scaling - focus on the protocores that give you flat atk+atk% substats primarily
    
- Special Note for Delta type Protocores:
    

- If you have Memories (Cards) that match the battle’s stellacrum requirements, then build with damage to weakness as the main stat for delta protocores
    
- If you have Memories (Cards) that DO NOT match the battle’s stellacrum requirements aka you are attempting to brute force the battle, then build with crit rate or crit dmg will be the main stat for delta protocores
    

Battle Preference (Boss / Mob): Boss
Weapon Preference: Guns, Claymore [link to weapon guide] 
The weapon you choose will be tied to the Active Skill in battle.

Battle Rotation: Execute the standard rotation that applies to boss unless you need to watch out for specific boss mechanics (refer our linked boss guides for specific enemies)

Resonance Skill > Active skill > BA or CA (depending on weapon used) > Resonance Skill (for complete shield break) > Ardent Oath
