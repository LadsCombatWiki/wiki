## General
- **LI** - love interest.
- **OG3** - refers to original 3 men: Xavier, Rafayel, Zayne.
- **RNG** - “random number generation” except no one actually calls it that. Basically just the randomness of anything (trust us, this game’s combat has way too much RNG to explain here). A “good RNG run” means that lady luck was on your side and you got lots of high damage numbers.
## Companion kit
- **Companion** - this is who you fight alongside with in battles.
- **Free companion** - the game gives free 4* companions for all LI.
- **Myth companion** - 5* companion that needs to be unlocked from gacha.
- **Kit** - general term for the sum of all the skills a companion can do
- **Rotation** - the specific order of different attacks. When optimised, will do the maximum damage
- **Button mashing** - refers to repeatedly tapping an attack without consideration for rotation
- **Cooldown (CD)** - some actions need to be charged over time before they can be used again, namely the active, resonance and support skills
- **Buff** - a positive effect during battle from abilities or other sources.
- **Debuff** - a negative effect that can harm or decrease stats.
- **AoE** - “area of effect”; used to describe an attack that can cause damage over a region instead of just one specific enemy / single target.
- **DoT** - “damage over time”; used to describe mechanics that cause HP loss not from direct hits, but from debuff or AoE zone.
- **Crowd Control** - an ability some companions may have that help manage mobs.
## Team and Cards
- **Lunar Card** - has a crescent moon icon; a maximum of 4 can be equipped in a team.
- **Solar Card** - has the “sun” icon; a maximum of 2 can be equipped in a team.
- **Solar Pair** - two solar cards that are matched; pairs are represented by *two letters*.
- **Myth** - a 5* solar pair; unlocks a 5* companion. 
	- **Standard Myth** - can be obtained in all banners and from crates in XSpace Echo; cannot be obtained in Galaxy Explorer or wishing well shop.
	- **Limited Myth** - can only be obtained in limited banner runs and are usually stronger than standard myths.
- **Card talent** - each card has a talent that increases d2w; can scale of ATK/HP/ATK.
- **Rank** - additional copies of a card. R0, R1, etc
- **Pair Bonus** - when solar pairs are equipped, they will activate a bonus based on their rank. Ranked 5* solar pairs result in additions to the kit.
	- **R-1** - is used in reference to a 5* companion used on a team that has not equipped the companions solar pair.
>[!WARNING]- Ranked solar pair
>A solar pair is only considered ranked when **both** cards have been ranked to the same level. If they are not the same, the rank of the pair will be the same as the card with the lowest rank -image of matching vs lopsided solar pairs + description-

## Battle
- **D-Pad** - “directional pad”; is on the left side of the screen and controls the movement of MC
- **Basic Attack (BA)** - tap the weapon icon
- **Charged Attack (CA)** - hold the weapon icon
- **Dodge (dash)** - special charge/dash when you can avoid damage from enemy's attack and move a little bit backward.
	- **Direct dodge (dash)** - dodge in the direction dictated by the D-Pad.
	- **Perfect dodge** - tapping the dash/dodge button just as an enemy is able to hit you. This will trigger an empowered support skill.
- **Animation cancel** - interrupt MC animation attacks with dodge.
- **Energy** - allows you to use skills. Charges over time and can be sped up with eeb cores. 3 charges is the standard.
- **Active Skill (active)** - usually consumes 1 energy charge to deal a special attack
- **Frangere/Frang** - [[Claymore guide|hunter claymore's]] active skill when you have the full 3 stacks.
- **Resonance Skill (reso)** - usually consumes 2 energy charges to deal a special joint attack that can break shields.
- **Ardent Oath (oath)** - consumes the oath charge to deal an “ultimate” attack with your companion.
- **Support skill** - your companion’s icon to command him to perform his special attack.
	- **Empowered support** - when you trigger a perfect dodge, your companion’s support will become fully charged and deal special damage if used when it is flashing.
## Stats
- **Base stat** - a card’s fixed stats. The black stats in card description.
- **Mainstat** - the top most line on a core.
- **Substat** - the minor stats on a core that increase every 3 level of core.
- **Scaling stat** - ATK/HP/DEF that the companion uses the most to deal damage.
- **Normal damage** - indicated by **white** damage numbers.
- **Critical hit (crit/critting/crit hit)** - when you deal additional damage, indicated by **orange** damage numbers.
- **Crit rate (cr, crate)** - a percentage increase in the chance of dealing a critical hit.
- **Crit dmg (cd, cdmg)** - a percentage increase in the additional damage dealt in a critical hit.
- **Weakened enemy (weakened state/weakened/weakness)** - when a shielded enemy loses its shield due to resonance skills, indicated by **Blue/Purple** damage numbers
- **DMG boost to weakened (d2w)** - stat that increases the amount of damage you deal to a weakened enemy.
- **Expedited Energy Boost (eeb/ee)** - speeds up the charge rate of energy stars
- **Oath Recovery Boost (orb)** - speeds up the charge rate of ardent oath
- **Oath’s Strength (os)** - increases the damage of the ardent oath
- **Roll** - a term used to describe increasing the levels, usually of a substat, on a core.
